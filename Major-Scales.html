What is a major scale?

  • W W H W W W H

Practice techniques

  • Play each mode of scale of major scale up and down, root at low and high
  • Ex. key of C maj.
    • C D E F G A B C B A G F E D C
    • D E F G A B C D C B A G F E D
    • ...
    • repeat starting at higher C octave moving down and back up
